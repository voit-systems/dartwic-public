Tiers help explain why two DARTWIC environments may expose different capabilities even when they look similar at first glance.

# Free

Best for evaluation, small experiments, or constrained workflows.

# Pro

Best when teams need broader day-to-day operator and integration features.

# Enterprise

Best when the environment needs the widest deployment, control, or organizational support surface.

# Enforcement

Treat tiers as capability boundaries, not just pricing labels. If a documented feature is unavailable, check tiering alongside compatibility and licensing.
